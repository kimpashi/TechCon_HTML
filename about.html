History

TechCon began in 2010 as a small gathering of passionate technologists. Over the years, it has grown into a global event with thousands of attendees.

Mission

Our mission is to connect innovators, promote cutting-edge ideas, and support a global community of technology enthusiasts.

Past Speakers

TechCon has hosted a diverse group of speakers, including:

  • Ada Lovelace – Known for her pioneering work in computing theory.
  • Alan Turing – Revolutionized modern computer science.
  • Grace Hopper – Developed the first compiler for a programming language.
